Good Old-Fashioned Pancakes

Description

With just few ingredients and only within short amount of time you can make delicious fluffy pancakes.

Ingredients

  • Eggs 3x
  • Flour 2x cups
  • Sugar 1x table spoon
  • Baking soda 1x table spoon
  • Milk or water 1x cup

Steps

  1. Add 2x cups of flour into large bowl
  2. Add 3x eggs, 1x table spoon of sugar, 1x table spoon of baking soda,and milk or water
  3. Stir everything until substance is liquid like
  4. Start frying on pan using vegetable oil each side for 2 min and pancake ready.
