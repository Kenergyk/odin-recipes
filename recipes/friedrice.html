Fried rice recipe

Description

This Fried Rice Redcipe is very easy to make and only takes 30-40 minutes to prepare.Convenient for people who have busy schedules and enjoy quick delicious meals.

Ingredients

  • Rice 2 cups
  • Green onion 1 pack
  • Mushrooms 2 pieces
  • Salt 1 tablespoon
  • Gochujang pasta 1 large spoon
  • Eggs 3x

Steps

  1. Cook 2 cups of clean rice in rice cooker for 30 min
  2. Fry green onion, 2 pieces of minced mushrooms
  3. Add salt and 1 large spoon of gochujang pasta
  4. After frying for 3 mins, add rice and 3 eggs and fry for 4 more minutes everything
