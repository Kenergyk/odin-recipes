Pasta

Description

Delicious pasta that anyone can make and which you can enjoy with red wine.

Ingredients

  • Minced meat 200g
  • Pasta noodles
  • Salt
  • Basil and tomato sauce
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Cut onion and mince to small size
  2. Add minced meat into the pan and fry for 20 min
  3. Add salt, basil and tomato sauce and water
  4. Boil for 20 more minutes
  5. At the same time boil pasta noodles for 10 min in a pot
  6. Add pasta filling to the noodles, once done add freshly cut tomatoes on top.
